Deluxe Suite

Indulge in luxury with our spacious deluxe suites. These elegantly appointed rooms boast a separate living area and a king-size bed for ultimate comfort. Enjoy breathtaking views of the city skyline from the large windows.

Family Room

Perfect for families, our family rooms offer ample space and convenience. These rooms feature a combination of queen and twin beds, accommodating up to four guests. Enjoy the privacy of separate sleeping areas and a cozy sitting area.

Executive Room

Designed with business travelers in mind, our executive rooms offer a dedicated workspace and enhanced amenities. Enjoy a comfortable king-size bed, a spacious work desk, and complimentary access to our executive lounge for added convenience.

Penthouse Suite

Experience the epitome of luxury in our exquisite penthouse suite. Spread across two floors, this opulent accommodation offers panoramic views of the city. Enjoy a private terrace, a separate living area, a grand dining room, and a master bedroom with a luxurious king-size bed.

Standard Room:

Our cozy and comfortable standard rooms are designed to provide a relaxing atmosphere. They feature a plush queen-size bed and modern amenities, ensuring a restful stay for our guests.

Honeymoon Suite

Celebrate your special occasion in our luxurious honeymoon suite. This romantic retreat features a beautifully decorated bedroom with a king-size bed and a private balcony. Unwind in the whirlpool tub for a truly indulgent experience.
